# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and behavior, not people.
- Accept different viewpoints and technical tradeoffs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and community discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
